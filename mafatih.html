Mafatih

Our application’s Mafatih section is divided into 4 categories
Duas, A’amal, Ziarah, Salat
You can make your personal Mafatih by choosing your favorite items in each section and have an easy access in My Mafatih
A search bar is also available so that you may search all sections of Mafatih for your favorite items. Furthermore, the dhikr of the day is displayed in the Dhikr bar.
The comprehensive Shia Muslim App provides every Muslim with various unique
features necessary for daily life.
